If you have recently moved within the same county in Texas, you can update your voter registration online:

Update your registration address (after moving within the same county)

To use this form, you will need:
  1. Your Texas driver's license or ID card
  2. Your social security number
  3. Your Voter Registration Card VUID (Voter Unique Identifier) Number
If you have moved between counties, then you will need to mail in a new voter registration form to update your voter registration. We can help by mailing you the form along with a pre-stamped return envelope, or (if you have a printer, stamp, and envelope) providing a form you can download and print yourself:

(Re-)Register to vote

Maybe you're still moving, unpacking the boxes, or dealing with one of the many important things that come along with moving. Re-registering to vote is one of those important tasks that you don't want to lose track of. The good news is we can make it really easy! In fact, you can do it right now.
